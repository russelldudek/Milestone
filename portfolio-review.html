Decision-led portfolio review

Use one copy per workstream, then aggregate exceptions and cross-workstream tradeoffs for the steering forum. Illustrative structure; adapt to client quality, data, privacy, and governance standards.

Review fieldEvidence / updateDecision implication
Outcome and value
Owner · baseline · measure		What changed in the value hypothesis? What evidence exists? What remains an assumption?Advance, reframe, or stop the outcome commitment
Data/model readiness
Access · quality · evaluation · monitoring		What condition is now true? Which gap blocks the next stage? Who owns remediation?Proceed with controls, hold, or escalate
Human and quality authority
Use · override · exception · approval		Who may rely on the capability? Where is human review mandatory? What evidence does quality require?Clarify authority before expanding scope
Spend and forecast
Actual · forecast · range · trigger		What changed, why, and what decision changes the forecast?Maintain, reallocate, reduce, or request sponsor decision
Dependencies
Team · vendor · system · data · control		Which dependency is on the critical path? What is the next action, owner, and due date?Re-plan sequence, escalate, or accept risk
Adoption ownership
User · workflow · enablement · sustainment		What will change in real R&D work? Who owns use and learning after delivery?Continue, narrow, or pause until ownership exists
